Our Story

Founded in 2024 May by agricultural visionary Srinu Naik Katravath, Next Afield began as a small initiative to bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and modern technology. Today, we've grown into a leading agri-tech solutions provider, serving thousands of farmers across multiple countries with our innovative approaches to sustainable agriculture.

Our Mission

To empower farmers and agricultural stakeholders by providing innovative, sustainable, and client-focused agricultural services and solutions. We aim to enhance productivity, promote environmentally friendly practices, and foster long-term partnerships through personalized support and cutting-edge technologies.

Our Vision

Agriculture Services and Solutions!

At Next Afield, we are more than just an agricultural service provider; we are a catalyst for change, committed to redefining the agricultural landscape. Our mission is to empower farmers, agribusinesses, and stakeholders by fostering sustainable, innovative, and profitable practices. Through a combination of tradition and cutting-edge technology, we offer a wide range of services designed to optimize operations, enhance productivity, and promote long-term sustainability. Our approach is data-driven and tailored to meet the diverse needs of modern agriculture. We specialize in precision agriculture, leveraging data analytics, GPS-guided equipment, and real-time monitoring through drones and satellites to enable informed decision-making. Our irrigation solutions focus on efficient water management through smart irrigation systems and practices that conserve resources. Sustainability is at the core of our services, with a focus on soil health, integrated pest management, and the adoption of renewable energy. We also provide comprehensive crop and farm management support, including strategic planning, supply chain management, and expert consulting on regulatory compliance and market trends. In addition to our core services, we offer a market price tracking system, integrating with platforms like Digital Mandi, to keep our clients informed on market fluctuations. Our research and development initiatives ensure that our clients stay ahead with the latest technologies and practices. Our commitment extends to training and capacity building, offering workshops, on-farm training, and online resources. We also facilitate local to global transportation and provide crop insurance and financial services to ensure the stability and growth of our clients' operations.
